# sevennortheast


Create a seamless 8-second looping animation of a minimalist compass emblem centered on a solid black background. The compass consists of an elegant eight-point star in bone white (#F5F1E8) with an inner compass needle in deep burgundy (#7F2524).

The burgundy needle rotates in 45° increments, pointing precisely to one of the eight star points every second. It begins pointing North (12 o'clock), then moves clockwise to North-East, East, South-East, South, South-West, West, North-West, and finally returns smoothly to North, completing a perfect loop.

The motion should be crisp, precise, and mechanically smooth, resembling the movement of a premium compass. Each transition lasts approximately 0.2–0.3 seconds, followed by a brief 0.7–0.8 second hold before moving to the next point. There should be no jitter, wobble, overshoot, or easing bounce—only clean rotational movement around the exact center pivot.

Maintain perfect symmetry throughout. The bone white star remains completely static while only the burgundy needle rotates. Use a flat, high-contrast, minimalist design with no textures, shadows, gradients, glow, or additional visual effects. The animation should feel elegant, refined, and timeless, with a seamless loop where the final frame matches the first exactly.
